Top 10 Video Game Characters

Here are the characters that have won the top 6-10! Enjoy! If you want to go back to the main page, Click here.

6. Donkey Kong


Game: Donkey Kong Country, Donkey Kong Country: Tropical Freeze, Tipping Stars
Abilities: Donkey Kong is very strong. He can use Strong KOng by entering a barrel with his face on it. Sometimes he can clap and create soundwaves.
Enemies: Manky Kong, Chomps, Kritter, Rockkroc, Klaptrap
Creators & Developers: Shiergu Miyamoto, Gunpei Yokoi, Atari, Nintendo, Retro Studios
Released: July 9, 1981

7. Pikachu


Game: Pikachu Games 2017, Detective Pikachu, Battle Revolution, Puzzle League, Pokemon Sun & Moon
Abilities: Pikachu is an electric type. Some of its attacks are Thunder Jolt, Quick Attack, and Spark.
Enemies: Blaine, Cilan, Domino, James, Jessie, Meowth, Gary Oak, Giovanni
Creators & Developers: Satoshi Tajiri, Ken Sugimori, Atsuko Nishida, Game Freak, Pokemon
Released: 1996

8. Link


Game: Legends of Zelda, Majora's Mask, The Wind Waker, Four Swords Adventures, Twilight Princess
Abilities: When Link was a child he used a bow & arrow. He now uses a powerful sword to fight off his enemies.
Enemies: Armos, Blade Trap, Octorok, Iron Knuckle, Anti-Fairy, Freezor
Creators & Developers: Nintendo, Shigeru Miyamoto, Akito Nakatsuka, Takashi Tezuka
Released: January 14, 1987

9. Bendy


Game: Bendy & The Ink Machine
Ablities: He can shoot ink at you to temporarily blind you as you are fighting him. He can draw toon weapons with his tail and use them to fight back.
Enemies: Alice Angel, Sammy Lawrence, he is also the villain so you play against him.
Creators & Developers: TheMeatly Games
Released: February 10, 2017

10. Mega Man


Game: Mega Man Powered Up, Mega Man 11, Rockman EXE WS, Rockman Strategy, Mega Man Xtreme
Abilities: He can shoot blasts out of his arm as his main weapon. There is many power ups in the game he uses to acquire specific powers.
Enemies: Crazy Razy, Killer Bullet, Sniper Joe, Tackle Fire, Watcher, Yellow Devil
Creators & Developers: Keiji Inafune, Tokuro Fujiwara, Capcom, Inti Creates, Noritaka Funamizu
Released: December 17, 1987
