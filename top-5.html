Top 5

Here are some amazing facts about our favorite video game characters! If you want to return to the main page, Click here.

1) Kirby


Game: Kirby Star Allies, Kirby Battle Royale, Kirby Blowout Blast
Enemies: King Dedede, Shadow Kirby, Sword Knight
Abilities: He can swallow his enemies and copy their powers.
Creators & Developers: Nintendo, Masahiro Sakurai, Satoru Iwata
Created: October 24th, 2011

2) Pac-Man


Game: Pac-Man
Enemies: Pinky, Blinky, Inky, Clyde
Abilities: He can change his appearance and have special powers with different power berries.
Creators & Developers: Atari, Namco, Interactive Brains, Toru Itwani
Created: May 22, 1980

3) Mario & Luigi


Game: Dream Team, Mario Kart, Super Mario, Super Smash Bros, Paper Jam, Superstar Saga
Enemies: Bowser, Goomba, Bowser Jr., Shy Guy, Chain Chomp, Wario, Waluigi, Boomerang Bro
Abilities: He can get different power ups like the star, and different types of flowers, that give him special abilities.
Creators & Developers: Nintendo, Shigeru Miyamoto, Atari, Sega, UbiSoft
Created: 1981

4) Jiggly Puff


Game: Pokemon Pinball, Pokemon Snap, Pokemon COnquest, Pokemon Mystery Dungeon
Enemies: Any other Pokemon Abilities: It can sing and make other Pokemon and people fall asleep. Some of Jiggly Puff's attacks are Hypnotic Gaze, Sing, and Double Slap.
Creators & Developers: Ken Sugimori, Pokemon
Created: 1996

5) Sonic


Game: Sonic Mania, Sonic Runners, Sonic Boom, Sonic Lost World, Super Smash Bros
Enemies: Dr. Eggman, Orbot, Metal Sonic, Chaos, Metal Knuckles, Zavok, Zazz
Abilities: He runs very fast! He can curl up into a ball and roll very fast too!
Creators & Developers: Sega, Nintendo, Gameloft, Hirokazu Yasuhara, Yuji Naka
Created: June 1991
